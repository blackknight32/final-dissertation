Audi


Audi AG is a German Automotive manufacturer of luxury vehicles headquartered in Ingolstadt,Germany.As a subsidairy of its parent company, the volkswagen Group,Audi produces vehicles in nine production facilities worldwide.The brand that specializes in precision,performance & perfection was founded by Sir August Horch in 1909 with the introduction of the F103 series,thus creating the present day icon.The company is based on the Latin translation of the surname of the founder,August Horch meaning "listen" becomes audi in latin.The slogan "Vorsprung Durch Technik", meaning "Being Ahead through Technology" was proved by winning multiple le mans titles through 1999 and rest lies the history.....
